Takoyaki

this is how to make some takoyaki.

when you are done, the Takoyaki will be made.

Required skills

proficiency with chopsticks

Ingredients

  • ball-shaped fryer
  • oil (preferred type)
  • batter
  • octopus
  • shallots
  • (optional) mayonnaise and tonkatsu sauce

Steps

  1. heat up the fryer
  2. coat fryer in preferred oil
  3. fill ball-shaped holes with batter (time sensitive or else your batter may burn)
  4. before hardened, place in octopus and other ingredients with chopsticks
  5. wait for the takoyaki batter to harden and then carefully flip the ball onto its other side to cook each side evenly
  6. once golden, turn off fryer. carefully remove takoyaki with chopsticks and serve
